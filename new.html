About Us

Welcome to the OWASP Chapter of MANIT, Bhopal! We are excited to announce the launch of our new chapter dedicated to improving software and cybersecurity awareness among students.

Building a secure digital community starts here!

At our chapter, we believe in starting with the basics of cybersecurity and gradually progressing to more advanced topics through workshops, seminars, and meetups.

Join us to level up your security skills!
