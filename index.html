Mission Statement

At OWASP MANIT, we are committed to empowering students with the knowledge and skills to combat cyber threats. Our mission is to foster a culture of cybersecurity awareness, promote secure coding practices, and build a resilient community of future cybersecurity leaders.

Identity

Welcome to the OWASP Chapter of MANIT, Bhopal! We are a student-led society dedicated to improving software and cybersecurity awareness among our peers.

Through workshops, seminars, and collaborative projects, we aim to educate and empower the MANIT community, creating a safer digital environment for all.
